Class: Cocktail

Attributes:  
glass_type (string)  
alcohol (string)  
mixers (array)  
with_ice (boolean)  
garnish (string)  
price (float)  

Methods:  
on_the_rocks (set with_ice to true)  
neat (sets with_ice to false)  
make_virgin (changes the alcohol attribute to a blank string)  
comp (changes the price attribute to 0.00)
